Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before Code. Connect. Create.

What exactly does my ticket include?+

Every ticket covers full access to the main stage, all breakout tracks, and the workshop sessions listed under your plan, plus meals and coffee breaks across all three days. Standard includes 2 workshops, Pro unlocks unlimited workshop access.

Can I attend remotely if I can't travel to the venue?+

DevConf 2026 is an in-person-first event designed around hallway conversations and live workshops, so we don't offer a livestream this year. Every Pro and Team ticket does include recordings so you can catch any session you miss.

What's the refund policy if my plans change?+

Cancel up to 30 days before the event for a full refund, or transfer your ticket to a colleague at any time for free. Inside the 30-day window, tickets can still be transferred but are no longer refundable.

Do you offer discounts for students or groups?+

Students get 50% off the Standard plan with a valid .edu email or student ID. Bringing more than 10 people? The Team plan already bundles 10 seats, and we'll happily quote a custom rate for larger squads.

Where is the venue and how do I get there?+

DevConf 2026 takes place at Tech City Convention Center, a 15-minute shuttle ride from the airport. Shuttle pickup times and a full venue map are emailed to every attendee one week before the event.
